About

We use in situ technology.
We create in situ technology.
We love in situ technology.

precipitate.bio came out of need. In situ technology is confusing and fickle. (Is DNA-Paint different from Oligopaint? Answer: Yes.) While working on FISSEQ technology, I was inspired by the amazing pace of technology advancement in imaging single molecules inside cells and tissues. However, keeping up with the state of the field was almost a job in itself! And often, when I actually used one of these methods, the “methods” section was far from a complete protocol. I hope precipitate.bio is the place to go for critical insights and advice, a trustworthy knowledge base, the friend who pings you to ask if you’ve seen that new paper, and the reviewer who suggests that one more experiment — in short, my goal is to create a community of in situ technology enthusiasts.

— Evan R. Daugharthy, precipitate.bio co-founder
